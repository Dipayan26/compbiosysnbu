ABOUT

Established in 2019, our Computational Systems Biology Lab is part of the Department of Bioinformatics at the University of North Bengal. We engage in interdisciplinary research, bridging biological and computational sciences to address complex biological challenges. Our lab applies computational tools and techniques to analyze and model intricate biological processes, providing insights into various domains such as multi-omics data integration, biological network modeling, and disease research.

We strive to equip students and researchers with essential computational skills, fostering expertise in algorithm development, data analysis, and software tools for solving real-world biological problems.
