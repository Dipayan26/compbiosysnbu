Lab Functionality

In Computational system biology lab we mainly focus on developing algorithm, webtools, software for the enhancement of the usage of computational techniques in the field of biological research. We are working on different aspect of biological network modelling based on the resources available in the public domain. Different environmental factors causing disease conditions imprint their effect on the biological systems. Integrating different level of data like genomic, proteomic, metabolomic lead to the deep insight into the disease study which helps to understand the disease with target specific therapeutics. Such study not only computationally challenging but also seeks a lot of computational techniques. We develop algorithm based on computational techniques like machine learning, statistical modelling.
